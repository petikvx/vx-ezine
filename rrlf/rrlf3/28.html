thoughts by disk0rdia

One morning, when I woke up
with a smile in my blouchy eyes
I saw the beauty of the yellow sun,

She burned happyness in my empty mind
and so I went out to kill all the love in
the world
The happyness in my mind was driving
me crazy, the love makes me sad
I didn't know why
So I say goodbye to the happyness,
the yellow sun and the terribly love
in the world
